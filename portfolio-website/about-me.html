About Me

Hi, I’m Gabriel Laboy — a senior Computer Science student at UMass Amherst with a passion for creative problem-solving through code and design. My interests span web development, game design, and system administration, and I’m always eager to explore the intersection of technology and user experience.

Over the past few years, I’ve built a diverse skill set through hands-on roles in research, software development, and IT. I currently work at SureScan Corporation supporting machine learning workflows with detailed image validation, and I serve as a Resident Assistant where I foster community and lead with empathy. My past internships and research experience have helped me develop responsive websites, automate data pipelines, and collaborate effectively in both technical and community-oriented environments.

Outside of academics and work, I’ve built a personal homelab to deepen my understanding of system infrastructure, and I’ve created interactive browser games and Unity projects that reflect my love for playful, impactful design.

Feel free to check out my portfolio, explore my games on itch.io, or browse my code on GitHub.
